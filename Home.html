Data Analytics Research Lab

Data Analytics Research LAB

The Data Analytics Research Group, formed in 2021, is led by Dr. Vishal Krishna Singh. The group focuses on research in data analytics in smart systems, specially, Ad-Hoc Networks, Sensor Networks, Internet of Things, and Big Data Networking. We are a group of enthusiastic learners from diverse research background and specialization in different domains of analytics. We welcome learners and research enthusiasts from all over the world, to collaborate with us and contribute to the experience and perspectives of the group.
